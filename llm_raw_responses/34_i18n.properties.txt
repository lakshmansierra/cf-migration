Your plan is to copy the internationalization resource bundle from i18n\i18n.properties to webapp/i18n/i18n.properties for your SAPUI5 application. Here’s how you can do it:

**Step-by-step instructions:**

1. **Create the target directory (if it doesn’t exist):**
   - Make sure the folder webapp/i18n exists in your project structure.

2. **Copy the file content:**
   - Create a file named i18n.properties inside webapp/i18n.
   - Paste the following content into webapp/i18n/i18n.properties:

```properties

```

**Result:**
- Your SAPUI5 app will now use these texts for internationalization, making them available for binding in your UI5 views and controllers.
